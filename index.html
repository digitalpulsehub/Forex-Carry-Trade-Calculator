Carry Trade Strategy

A carry trade is a strategy where an investor borrows money in a currency with a low interest rate and invests it in a currency with a higher interest rate.

  • Funding: Short the low-yield currency.
  • Profit: Earn the interest differential (Swap).
  • Risk: Exchange rate volatility is the primary risk factor.
