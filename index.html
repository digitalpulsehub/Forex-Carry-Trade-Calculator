Carry Trade Strategy

The carry trade involves borrowing in a currency with a low interest rate and investing in a currency providing a higher rate of return.

  • Funding: Low-yield currency (e.g. JPY).
  • Investment: High-yield currency (e.g. USD).
  • Risk: Currency fluctuations can offset interest gains.
