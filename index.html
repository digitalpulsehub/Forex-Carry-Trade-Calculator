How Carry Trade Works

The Carry Trade strategy involves borrowing a currency with a low interest rate (Funding Currency) to purchase a currency with a higher interest rate (Asset Currency).

  • The Goal: Capture the "Interest Rate Differential".
  • Daily Earnings: You earn the difference (swap) every day the position is held overnight.
  • The Risk: If the high-yield currency devalues against the low-yield one, the capital loss can exceed the interest gained.
